Atmospheric spatial system exploring wind, pressure, and environmental movement.

2B DESIGN STUDIO
ENVIRONMENTS II
The Atmospheric Circuit is a vertical terrain designed as a 1:1 simulation of a rugged, rocky ascent for the high-endurance runner. The project investigates the contradiction between geological mass and digital infrastructure, where a primordial cave experience is facilitated by hyper-engineered systems. At its foundation, a rectangular wind catcher drives air through a subterranean earth tube network, leveraging stable ground temperatures to pre-cool the atmosphere. The runner’s journey is defined by the friction between the jagged, cave-like interior and a decentralized network of servers. The ascent culminates beneath a skylight, grounding the runner in natural light within a hyper-engineered void.
