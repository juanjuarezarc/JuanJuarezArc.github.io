AIR

Atmospheric spatial system exploring wind, pressure, and environmental movement.

2B DESIGN STUDIO
ENVIRONMENTS II
The Atmospheric Circuit is a vertical terrain designed as a 1:1 simulation of a rugged ascent. The project investigates the contradiction between geological mass and digital infrastructure. A wind catcher drives air through subterranean systems. The ascent culminates beneath a skylight, creating a controlled rupture between systems.
